## Projects

### AlphaSurface

Real-time voice + vision AI co-thinker on an infinite canvas. No chat box — Think Mode places sticky notes that challenge your logic directly on the canvas; Explain Mode converts speech into connected visual nodes. Six parallel sub-agents running underneath.

`React` `tldraw` `FastAPI` `WebSockets` `Google ADK` `Gemini 2.5` `Cloud Run` `Firestore`

### Crucible

Socratic idea interrogation engine. Refuses to generate a plan until it has stress-tested the riskiest assumption in your pitch. Builds a persistent visual map of your idea in React Flow, traces every decision back to its source assumptions, then delivers a Go/No-Go verdict.

`React` `React Flow` `Framer Motion` `shadcn/ui` `FastAPI` `Gemini API` `Supabase`
